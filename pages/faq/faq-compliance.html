Admissions Compliance.

Important information on how we verify academic qualifications, conduct background checks, and adhere to strict safeguarding and immigration frameworks.

We inspect original certificates or request official direct transcripts from examination boards (Pearson, AQA, OCR, etc.) to verify your GCSE, A-Level, BTEC, or international qualifications.
We verify all academic and professional references directly through the referee's official school or college email domain to ensure legitimacy and academic integrity.

Applicants must provide a chronological timeline of their education and work history. Any gaps of more than 3 months are reviewed by our advisors to help present a strong personal narrative in your statement.

Yes. We run identity checks on all applicants. For international candidates, we advise on the requirements for obtaining a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) number and Student Visa.
Our international admissions desks map global high school and degree transcripts to UK equivalents using official UK ENIC conversion frameworks.
We pre-screen applicants for Student Loans Company eligibility, ensuring they meet the required UK residency, nationality, and previous-study criteria before applying.
All student documents and personal data are encrypted and hosted on secure cloud servers in strict compliance with the UK Data Protection Act and GDPR.

We verify IELTS, TOEFL, or Duolingo English Test report forms directly with test centers to ensure applicant language scores satisfy university requirements.
