1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

1.1. KENSINGTON IVY LTD (Companies House No. 17167406) is committed to ensuring that our website, kensingtonivy.co.uk, is accessible to the widest possible audience, including individuals with disabilities, impairments, or those using assistive technologies.

1.2. We believe that everyone — regardless of ability — should be able to access information about our services, register as a candidate, and contact us without encountering barriers. Accessibility is not an afterthought; it is a core part of how we design and maintain our digital presence.

1.3. This statement applies to the Kensington Ivy website at kensingtonivy.co.uk and all sub-pages within that domain. It does not apply to third-party websites linked from our site.

2. Conformance Status

2.1. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

2.2. Our current conformance status is: Partially conformant. This means that some content does not yet fully conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA, as described in the Known Limitations section below. We are actively working to address these gaps.

2.3. We have implemented the following accessibility features across our website:

  • Semantic HTML5 elements used throughout (header, nav, main, aside, footer) to ensure correct document structure for screen readers.
  • All images include descriptive alt attributes.
  • Colour contrast ratios meet WCAG 2.1 AA minimum requirements (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text).
  • All interactive elements (buttons, links, form fields) are keyboard-navigable and include visible focus indicators.
  • Forms include associated <label> elements for all inputs.
  • The website does not contain content that flashes more than three times per second.
  • Page language is declared (lang="en") to support screen reader language detection.
  • Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.

3. Known Limitations

3.1. Despite our efforts, we are aware of the following areas where our website does not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA:

  • PDF documents: Any PDF documents linked from the site may not be fully accessible to screen reader users. We are working to produce accessible HTML equivalents of all key documents.
  • Third-party embedded content: Some embedded tools or widgets provided by third parties (e.g., analytics or mapping integrations) may not meet accessibility standards. We audit third-party tools regularly and raise accessibility issues with our suppliers.
  • Complex navigation on mobile: Certain dropdown menu interactions may require refinement for users relying solely on keyboard navigation on mobile devices. This is a known issue we are addressing.

3.2. We are committed to resolving all known accessibility issues as part of our ongoing development cycle. We aim to address the items listed above by 31 December 2026.

4. Technical Information

4.1. Kensington Ivy relies on the following technologies for the rendering of our website:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript (ES6+)

4.2. Our website has been tested using the following tools and methods:

  • Manual keyboard navigation testing across all key page types.
  • Screen reader testing using NVDA (Windows) and VoiceOver (macOS/iOS).
  • Automated scanning using the WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool (wave.webaim.org).
  • Colour contrast checking using the WebAIM Contrast Checker (webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker).

5. Feedback & Alternative Formats

5.1. We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any barrier that prevents you from accessing any part of our content, or if you require information in an alternative format (e.g., large print, audio, Easy Read), please contact us:

  • Email: info@kensingtonivy.co.uk
  • Telephone: 020 7444 0009 (available Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm)
  • Post: Kensington Ivy Ltd, 453 South End Road, Hornchurch, England, RM12 5NX

5.2. We will respond to accessibility feedback within 5 working days and will work with you to provide the information you need in a format that is accessible to you.

6. Enforcement Procedure

6.1. If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility complaint, you have the right to contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS):

  • Website: equalityadvisoryservice.com
  • Telephone: 0808 800 0082

6.2. This statement was prepared on 6 May 2026 and will be reviewed annually, or sooner following any significant change to the website.
