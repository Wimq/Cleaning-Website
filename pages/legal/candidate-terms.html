1. The Terms

1.1. These Terms constitute the Agreement between Kensington Ivy Ltd and the student applicant ("the Student") and govern the university advisory and placement services provided.

1.2. This Agreement does not guarantee university placement, nor does it guarantee student funding or tuition loan approval, which are subject to individual university admissions decisions and Student Finance criteria.

1.3. Standard placement services are free of charge. Kensington Ivy does not charge students for standard course applications, profile mapping, or statement reviews.

2. Services

2.1. The Company will endeavour to provide academic profiling, qualification mapping, UCAS guidance, and placement advisory.

2.2. The Student agrees to provide accurate academic records, transcripts, reference details, and predicted/final grades.

2.3. The Student agrees to: (a) Provide truthful personal information; (b) Meet deadlines for application drafts; (c) Inform the Company of any direct university offers.

3. Qualification Verification & DBS

3.1. The Student acknowledges that universities require rigorous qualification audits and credit checks prior to enrollment.

3.2. The Student must disclose any academic suspensions, exclusions, or investigations by schools or colleges.

3.3. For courses requiring criminal record checks (e.g. Medicine, Teaching, Social Work), the Student is responsible for securing an Enhanced DBS Certificate. Kensington Ivy will assist in directing the Student to the appropriate application channels.

4. Fees & Student Funding

4.1. Standard placement advisory services are provided at no charge to the student.

4.2. Any premium coaching, mock admissions boards, or specialized portfolio reviews will be subject to a separate written fee agreement.

4.3. The Student is solely responsible for submitting tuition fee loan and maintenance loan applications to Student Finance England (SFE) or relevant bodies.

5. Term & Withdrawals

5.1. This Agreement begins upon registration and continues until the Student accepts a university placement or terminates the engagement.

5.2. The Student must notify Kensington Ivy in writing within 7 days if they decide to withdraw their university application or defer entry.

6. Admissions Regulations

6.1. The Student agrees to comply with UCAS application regulations and target university entry conditions.

6.2. Kensington Ivy acts as an advisor; the final contract of enrollment exists solely between the Student and the admitting higher education institution.

7. Confidentiality & Data Protection

7.1. The Student agrees that Kensington Ivy may process and share their transcripts, statements, and profiles with prospective universities for placement purposes.

7.2. Standard student data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

8. Intellectual Property

8.1. The Student retains copyright over their personal statements, though Kensington Ivy retains rights over any advisory material or templates supplied.

9. Admissions Integrity & Anti-Plagiarism

9.1. The Student explicitly agrees that all statements, writings, and portfolios submitted represent their own work. Kensington Ivy does not write statements on behalf of students and takes no responsibility for any plagiarism detection flags triggered.

10. Termination

10.1. Either party may terminate this agreement at any time by written notice.

11. Limitation of Liability

11.1. Kensington Ivy shall not be liable for any loss, delay, rejection, or withdrawal of university placement offers by higher education institutions.

11.2. The Student is advised to confirm all course criteria and tuition fee terms directly with the admitting university before final enrollment.
