The Reality of Results Day

When A-Level and IB results are released, thousands of students who missed their conditional offers immediately flood the UCAS system. Phone lines to university admissions departments become jammed, and the most desirable course vacancies are filled within a matter of hours.

Attempting this process alone means you are fighting against the clock, constantly refreshing the UCAS Hub, and struggling to advocate for yourself over a chaotic public hotline.

  • Severe bottlenecking on public university switchboards.
  • Limited visibility into unadvertised course vacancies.
  • Administrative errors during the "Add Clearing Choice" phase.

The Kensington Ivy Solution

We completely remove the burden from you. By retaining our services prior to Results Day, our senior consultants handle every aspect of the Clearing process on your behalf.

We do not use public channels. We utilize our established network of university registrars and admissions deans to bypass the noise and place you exactly where you belong.

Our 4-Phase Executive Protocol

1 Academic Triage & Contingency

Before the UCAS Hub officially updates, our team prepares a comprehensive contingency matrix. We map your predicted grades, your firm choices, and potential alternative pathways. The moment results are published, we perform an immediate audit of your UCAS Tariff points to determine your exact standing.

If you are released into Clearing, we instantly retrieve your UCAS Clearing ID without subjecting you to the delays of a crashing student portal.

What We Handle:

Complete audit of your A-Level/IB results against original conditional offers.

Real-time monitoring of your UCAS Hub status changes.

Immediate extraction of your unique Clearing Number.

2 Private Network Sourcing

While the general public relies on the standard UCAS Search tool—which is often delayed and missing crucial data—our consultants cross-reference your profile against our private database of university partners.

We identify unadvertised vacancies at Russell Group and elite institutions, analyzing which courses perfectly align with your long-term career aspirations, rather than just settling for what is publicly available.

What We Handle:

Direct communication with faculty to uncover hidden course availabilities.

Strategic matching based on your specific subject strengths and weaknesses.

3 Direct Admissions Negotiation

This is where our service provides the most value. We do not call public hotlines or speak to temporary call-center staff. Our senior consultants dial directly into the offices of Admissions Deans and Faculty Heads.

We present a compelling, professional case for your admission, highlighting your extracurricular achievements, specific module grades, and overall academic trajectory to negotiate a firm verbal offer on the spot.

What We Handle:

Bypassing public switchboards to reach decision-makers instantly.

Professional pitch outlining why you are an exceptional candidate.

Securing binding verbal offers and strict deadlines for portal entry.

4 Official Portal Lock-In

A verbal offer is only the beginning. The process must be meticulously finalized through the UCAS Hub. We guide you precisely through the "Add Clearing Choice" process, ensuring the institution code, course code, and campus details are entered without a single error.

Our team then monitors the backend until the university officially updates your status to 'Unconditional', permanently locking in your placement.

What We Handle:

Step-by-step guidance on entering the correct UCAS institution codes.

Verification that the university has formally accepted the clearing choice.
