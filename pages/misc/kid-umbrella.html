This document sets out key information about your student funding relationship with us and the lending body (Student Loans Company) used in your academic engagement. It is a statutory disclosure to provide this to you before enrollment.

General Information

Name of Admissions AdvisoryKENSINGTON IVY LTD
Funding Intermediary (Lender)Student Loans Company (SLC) / Student Finance England (SFE)
Type of FundingUndergraduate Student Loans Portfolio (Tuition & Maintenance)
Who will pay youStudent Finance England (direct to student for maintenance)
How often you will be paidThree installments per year (at the start of each academic term)

The Funding & Loan Structure

Kensington Ivy assists you in aligning your university application to secure entry. Student Finance England pays the approved Tuition Fee Loan directly to the university's registry. The Maintenance Loan is paid directly to your bank account to support living expenses.

Deductions from Tuition LoanNone (100% of the tuition fee loan is paid directly to the university to cover course fees)
Deductions from Maintenance LoanNone (100% of the approved maintenance loan is paid directly to your bank account; no taxes or service fees are deducted during your study)
