Our Primary Pathways

Our consultants analyze your exact UCAS tariff, subject mix, and career goals to assign you to one of our three core alternative entry frameworks.

Integrated Foundation Year

A 4-year undergraduate degree where 'Year 0' prepares you for the rigor of Year 1. Taught on the main university campus by university faculty. You apply for a single 4-year student visa and student finance loan.

Ideal Profile

Recent school leavers who achieved DDD to CCD at A-Level.

Key Benefit

No re-application needed for Year 1.

Access to HE Diploma

A highly intensive 1-year course taught at partnered colleges. It is widely accepted by almost all UK universities (including the Russell Group) as a direct equivalent to 3 A-Levels.

Ideal Profile

Mature students (19+) or those changing careers entirely.

Key Benefit

Fully funded via the Advanced Learner Loan.

International Year One

This pathway combines intensive English language support with Year 1 academic modules. Upon successful completion, you progress directly into Year 2 of your chosen Bachelor's degree.

Ideal Profile

International students requiring English language improvement.

Key Benefit

Saves one full year of international tuition fees.
