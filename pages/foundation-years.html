Alternative Entry Excellence

Not every student follows a traditional academic route. Standard A-Level or IB grades do not always reflect a student's true potential, ambition, or intellect. Recognizing this, Kensington Ivy has established robust partnerships with leading UK institutions that offer dedicated, fully integrated Foundation Years and Access to Higher Education diplomas.

These specialized programs allow you to spend an initial year building the foundational knowledge required for your specific degree. Upon successful completion of this preparatory year, you are guaranteed progression into Year 1 of your chosen undergraduate program at the same institution.

Our Primary Pathways

We analyze your current academic standing and career goals to place you in the perfect bridging program.

Integrated Foundation Year

A 4-year undergraduate degree where 'Year 0' prepares you for the rigor of Year 1. Perfect for students who missed their conditional offers but still want the full university campus experience immediately.

  • Guaranteed Year 1 entry
  • Taught on the main university campus
  • Full access to Student Finance

Access to HE Diploma

Designed specifically for mature students (19+) returning to education. A 1-year intensive course taught at partnered colleges that is widely accepted by UK universities as an equivalent to 3 A-Levels.

  • Fast-track 1-year duration
  • Accepted by Russell Group unis
  • Advanced Learner Loan eligible

International Year One

For international students looking to improve academic and English language skills while simultaneously completing the first year of their degree, accelerating their graduation timeline.

  • Combines English & Academics
  • Progress directly to Year 2
  • Dedicated international support
