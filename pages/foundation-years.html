Our Primary Pathways

Our senior consultants analyze your exact UCAS tariff, subject mix, and career goals to assign you to one of our three core alternative entry frameworks.

4 Year Integrated Visa

Integrated Foundation Year

The ultimate solution for students who missed their conditional offers but still desire the immediate "university experience" without leaving campus.

This is a 4-year undergraduate degree where 'Year 0' prepares you for the rigor of Year 1. It is taught on the main university campus by university faculty. You apply for a single 4-year student visa and student finance loan.

Ideal Profile

Recent school leavers who achieved DDD to CCD at A-Level.

Key Benefit

No re-application needed for Year 1. Automatic progression.

1 Year Fast-Track

Access to HE Diploma

Designed specifically for mature students returning to education, or those seeking a rapid pivot in subject choice without traditional A-Levels.

A highly intensive 1-year course taught at partnered colleges. It is widely accepted by almost all UK universities (including the Russell Group) as a direct equivalent to 3 A-Levels, allowing you to leapfrog standard entry requirements.

Ideal Profile

Mature students (19+) or those changing careers entirely.

Key Benefit

Fully funded via the Advanced Learner Loan (written off eventually).

Accelerated Progression

International Year One

The premier option for international students aiming to graduate on time without spending an extra year on a foundation course.

This pathway combines intensive English language support with Year 1 academic modules. Upon successful completion of the year, you progress directly into Year 2 of your chosen Bachelor's degree.

Ideal Profile

International students requiring English language improvement.

Key Benefit

Saves one full year of international tuition fees and living costs.
